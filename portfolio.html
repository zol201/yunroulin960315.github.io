Overview of the project

  • Problem: Flooding has persisted due to 1. inadequate levee height & 2. backwater effect ,rainwater frequently overtops levees and inundates nearby villages.
  • Impact: Residents continue to experience disruptions to daily life and concerns for their safety, while farmland repeatedly suffers crop damage and financial loss.
  • Proposed Solution: Dredge the river and use farmland (government & private) for flood detention.
  • Challenge: Original plan to divert polluted canal water into farmland was strongly opposed by farmers.
  • Final Solution: Only rainwater was retained in the farmland, reducing overall flood detention efficiency but addressing local concerns.
